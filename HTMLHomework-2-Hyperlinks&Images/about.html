About Canyon Matka

Welcome to Canyon Matka, a breathtaking natural wonder located just outside of Skopje, North Macedonia.
Known for its stunning landscapes, rich biodiversity, and a variety of recreational activities,
Matka Canyon is a beloved destination for both nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

Our Mission

At Canyon Matka, we strive to preserve the natural beauty and ecological significance of this unique region while making it accessible and enjoyable for visitors.
We are committed to promoting eco-friendly tourism and educating our guests on the importance of conservation.

Discover the Wonders of Matka

Matka Canyon is home to:

  • Diverse Wildlife: An array of flora and fauna, including rare species like the Balkan lynx and over 70 types of butterflies.
  • Picturesque Landscapes: Majestic cliffs, serene waters, and lush greenery.
  • Rich History: Ancient monasteries and caves that date back centuries, reflecting the cultural heritage of the area.

Activities We Offer

  • Kayaking: Paddle through the crystal-clear waters of the Treska River and experience the canyon from a unique perspective.
  • Hiking: Enjoy numerous trails, each offering stunning views of the rugged cliffs and the pristine wilderness.
  • Cave Adventures: Discover hidden caverns like Vrelo Cave, one of the deepest underwater caves in Europe.
  • Photography: Capture the magic of Matka's landscapes, especially at sunrise and sunset when the light transforms the scenery.

Join Us in Protecting Canyon Matka

We believe in sustainable tourism and encourage all our visitors to respect and protect the natural environment.
Through various initiatives, we work to minimize our environmental impact and educate the community about the importance of preserving this beautiful area for future generations.
