This is my daily makeup routine:

  • Step 1: Skin Preparation
    • Products I use:
      1. Face Cleanser
        • Gentle cleanser for my skin type (acne-prone, oily)
          • Currently using Geek & Gorgeous Jelly Joker Low pH Cleanser
      2. Sunscreen
        • Lightweight sunscreen for acne-prone/oily skin
          • Always wear sunscreen (summer/winter)
          • Always use enough sunscreen to cover along the length of three fingers
            • Apply that amount evenly on face and neck
              • Currently using BIORE UV Aqua Rich Water Essence SPF 50+ PA++++
      3. Moisturizer
        • Apply moisturizer after applying sunscreen
          • Apply evenly to whole face and neck
            • Currently using SKIN1004 Madagascar Centella Poremizing Light Gel Cream
  • Step 2: Foundation & Concealer
    • Products I use:
      1. Foundation
        • Apply a small amount
          • Blend evenly using a sponge
            • Currently using L'Oréal Paris Infallible 32 Hour Fresh Wear Foundation in shade 130
          • Set with translucent powder
      2. Concealer
        • Apply under eyes
        • Cover any spots/acne spots
        • Blend evenly with sponge
          • Currently using Maybelline New York Fit Me Liquid Concealer in shade 12
        • Set with powder
  • Step 3: Eye Makeup
    • Products I use:
      • Mascara
        • Apply on upper and lower lashes
          • My personal favorite one is from Essence - I Love Extreme Crazy Volume Mascara
      • Eyeliner
        • Line upper lash line
        • Wing the outer corner
  • Step 4: Blush & Bronzer
    • Products I use:
      • Blush
        • Apply on the apples of the cheeks
          • Blend well for a natural look
            • Currently using Essence Baby Got Blush
      • Bronzer
        • Apply along the hairline, jawline, and hollows of the cheeks
          • Blend to avoid harsh lines
            • Currently using Catrice Sun Glow Matt Bronzing Powder in shade 30
